This is a sample post demonstrating inline math like $e^{i\pi} + 1 = 0$ and display math:

$$
\int_{-\infty}^{\infty} e^{-x^2} \, dx = \sqrt{\pi}
$$

You can also reference equations inline, like $E=mc^2$, and use lists:

- $a^2 + b^2 = c^2$
- $\nabla \cdot \mathbf{E} = \frac{\rho}{\varepsilon_0}$

You can embed a Quiver diagram by pasting a q.uiver.app URL on its own line:
